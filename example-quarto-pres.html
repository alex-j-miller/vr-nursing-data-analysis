Instructions: RE Lit Presentation

  • Delete this slide once you understand your task.
  • Fill in this template with answers gleaned from your reading of your paper.
  • You are not reading and interpreting the whole paper. You are taking a first look at it and mining it for specific information.
  • I recommend reading the abstract, then focusing on the methods section, reviewing all figures, and also checking the results section as needed. This is an high-level overview, not a deep read.

The Big Question

  • Replace the title of this slide with a brief statement of the topic of your paper
  • List as a bullet point (or more than one, if your paper has multiple RE models) the scientific question that your paper tries to answer with a RE model. Try to write the question as simply as possible - in words a 6th-grader could understand!

If you would like to include a direct quote from the paper, you can do it like this – start with two greater-than signs, and then add the text you want to quote. It will be formatted like a block quote (check out the rendered version to see the effect).
